Ask your CFO anything.

Ledger CFO — Rivera's Landscaping Co.
JR
Can I afford to hire a second crew lead this month?
L
Based on your current cash flow, you can afford it — but timing matters. Your average monthly revenue over the last 3 months is $48,200, with payroll running at 41%. Adding a crew lead at ~$5,200/month would bring that to 52%, which is manageable but tight. I'd recommend waiting until after your next two invoices clear — you have $12,400 outstanding that should land within 10 days. Want me to model what this hire looks like over the next 90 days?
JR
Yes, and also — am I on track to hit $600k revenue this year?
L
At your current pace, you're projected to hit $571,000 by December — about 5% short of your goal. The gap is mostly Q3, which historically dips for landscaping. If you land just one more commercial contract this summer, you close the gap entirely. Want me to identify which of your current clients are most likely to expand their contract?
