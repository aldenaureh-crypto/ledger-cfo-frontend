1. Who This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Ledger CFO, including visitors to our website, waitlist signups, free trial users, and paying subscribers. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and business name when you sign up
  • Financial data you upload: Bank statements, profit and loss statements, invoices, or any other financial documents you choose to share with us for analysis
  • Conversation data: Your questions and our AI responses, retained based on your data retention preferences
  • Usage data: How you interact with the Service, including features used and session duration, collected to improve the product
  • Payment information: Billing details processed securely through our payment provider — we do not store your full card details

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Ledger CFO Service
  • To analyze your financial data and generate personalized insights at your request
  • To send you product updates, account notifications, and service-related communications
  • To process your payments and manage your subscription
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your financial data to train AI models that serve other users without your explicit consent.

4. Your Data Retention Preferences

You are in control of how long your data lives in our system. We offer two modes:

  • Session-only mode: Your uploaded documents and conversation history are permanently deleted at the end of each session. Nothing is retained between logins.
  • Persistent profile mode: Your data is retained so that Ledger CFO can build a financial profile of your business over time, providing increasingly personalized insights. You can switch to session-only mode or request full deletion at any time.

You can update your retention preference at any time from your account settings.

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial data with any third parties. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • AI infrastructure providers: Your data may be processed by our underlying AI model providers (such as Anthropic) solely to generate responses. These providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and do not use your data for their own purposes.
  • Payment processors: Your billing information is handled by our payment provider under their own privacy and security standards.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your financial data seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), secure storage, and access controls that limit who within our team can view your data. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If we become aware of a data breach that affects your information, we will notify you promptly via email.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data — we will process this within 30 days
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at theledgercfo@gmail.com.

8. Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how users interact with our website. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not share analytics data with advertisers.

9. Children's Privacy

Ledger CFO is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with their information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice in the Service at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the Service after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
